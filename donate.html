The Honor System


The Honor System is how FreeDesignCamp generates revenue. While we provide our service for free, we are supported by generous people all over the world who donate whatever they can. Freedesigncamp put those donations to good use by improving our courses and paying instructors a royalty for their work. Please consider donating even a small amount to help FreeDesignCamp cover expenses and such if you found any of our courses or our site helpful in general.
